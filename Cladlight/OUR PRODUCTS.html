The Smart Jacket

The smart jacket is a unique product that aims to increase visibility of bikers on our roads. From
it's name, it is smart in that with every turn you take i.e right or left, or brake, the same is
reflected on the jacket automatically. The jacket adopts wireless technology to transmit signals to
the motor bike. On top of that, a fashion twist is added on the jacket making it trendy. The
inclusion of a tracker by use of GPS will allow for bikers location to be tracked real time.
